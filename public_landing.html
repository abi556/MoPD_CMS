How the process works

A transparent four-step journey from submission to final resolution.

1

Submission

Provide detailed information and upload supporting documents regarding your concern.

2

Review

The MoPD administrative team validates your entry and assigns an investigation officer.

3

Investigation

Assigned personnel perform due diligence and gather necessary data internally.

4

Resolution

A final decision is communicated to the citizen with a formal resolution report.
